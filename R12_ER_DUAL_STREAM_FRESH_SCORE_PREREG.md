## 1. Question

The train-only canary establishes exact ordinal routing on 2,000 families held
out from fitting but generated by the old grammar. This score asks the narrower
and harder question:

> Does the same learned compiler acquire fresh episodic relation laws from a
> new neutral namespace and renderer grammar, ignore same-shaped irrelevant
> names, compile a complete source-deleted relation packet, and recurrently
> execute that packet through depths one to twelve?

The experiment does not test free-form language, arithmetic, planning, or
general reasoning.

## 2. Immutable initialization and budget

Every arm reconstructs the exact confirmed parent with canary seed
`1790361034717866861`, then loads the same hash-bound v1.2 trainable state.
The shared trainable-state digest must be identical before any fresh-board
update.

The complete/trainable/headroom parameter certificate remains
`185,532,296 / 11,129,504 / 14,467,704`. The relation motor and reader have zero
learned parameters. No architecture, parameter, optimizer, loss, batch, epoch,
or update difference is allowed between arms.

## 3. Equal-budget training arms

All arms receive 48,000 source-only rows, 12,000 four-view families, two
epochs, 3,000 updates, family batch eight, 32 rows per update, AdamW `2e-4`,
100-step warmup, cosine decay, and the same family order.

1. **Treatment:** unchanged source compiler targets.
2. **Family deranged:** relation rows rotate between matched
   `(cardinality, rule_count)` families while source bytes and every other
   target remain unchanged.
3. **Equality ablated:** every after-witness name is replaced by a fresh
   same-width `z.....` name while source shape, neutral namespace, offsets, and
   declared targets remain unchanged.

Training exposes source compiler fields only. It contains no final state,
answer, recurrent trajectory, development oracle, or confirmation oracle.

## 4. Development interventions

The treatment is evaluated once on 2,048 development rows from 512 unseen
families and four renderer compositions disjoint from training. Raw hard
predictions are retained for independent recomputation.

- **Complete alpha recoding:** every `z.....` name in both program and late
  query is bijectively renamed. All hard fields and pointers must be identical.
- **Distractor rotation:** only irrelevant rule/event/query names rotate.
  Semantic occurrences, offsets, and targets remain untouched. All hard fields
  and pointers must be identical.
- **Rule-storage reindex:** the four rule records rotate in physical storage.
  Packet, state, answer, and validity must be identical.
- **Full physical reindex:** all eighteen records reverse in storage. Packet,
  state, answer, and validity must be identical.
- **Source-free identity collapse:** every semantic and irrelevant `z.....`
  name becomes the same six-byte token while grammar, record counts, ordinals,
  and lengths remain. Exact joint outcome must be at most 10%.
- **Packet interventions:** relation derangement, cardinality mask, state reset,
  and query swap must execute exactly when the base packet is exact and must
  change every sensitive result.

No intervention receives target outcomes, executor feedback, retry, search, or
repair.

## 5. Immutable development gates

Every gate is conjunctive. No threshold may change after source freeze.

1. treatment packet, state, answer, and joint each at least 95%;
2. every semantic field overall at least 95%;
3. every active pointer class overall at least 95%;
4. minimum cardinality-specific joint at least 90%;
5. minimum depth-specific joint at least 90%;
6. minimum renderer-specific joint at least 90%;
7. non-bijective-family joint at least 95%;
8. treatment packet and joint each at least 50 percentage points above both
   matched fitted controls;
9. both matched controls at most 35% packet and joint;
10. source-free joint at most 10%;
11. alpha, distractor, rule-storage, and full-record invariance each exactly
    2,048/2,048;
12. all four packet interventions have nonempty eligible and sensitive sets,
    exact execution on every eligible row, and changed output on every
    sensitive row;
13. all arms share the exact qualified-canary initialization;
14. excluded confirmed-parent state remains byte-identical in every arm;
15. exact complete system remains below 200M with zero learned motor/reader;
16. outcome supervision remains zero; and
17. custody ends at exactly one development and zero confirmation reads.

The independent assessor must reconstruct every metric and gate from immutable
raw tensors with a separate list executor. A self-reported pass is invalid.

## 6. Custody and decision

The score source, tests, preregistration, assessor, and job must be committed
and pushed before one deterministic training seed is derived. Training must
finish and its checkpoint must be made read-only before an atomic exclusive
development ledger is created. Only then may `development.jsonl` be read.

Failure of any gate closes this version and leaves confirmation unopened. A
complete independent development pass authorizes one separately frozen
confirmation evaluator with unchanged weights and thresholds. It does not
authorize tuning, same-board repair, or a broad reasoning claim.

## 7. Honest claim boundary

A confirmed pass would establish bounded fresh episodic relation induction,
exact variable-cardinality routing, source-deleted recurrent composition,
explicit halt, query readout, and robustness to same-shaped irrelevant names.
Candidate enumeration, monotone ordinal routing, finite tensor validation, and
the recurrence loop remain architectural priors. General language grounding,
new program ontologies, arithmetic, self-generated decomposition, planning,
and unbounded reasoning would remain open.
